01 — About

Who is frkudn?

I'm Furkan Uddin — a self-taught developer who codes on his own terms. No company, no boss, no sprint deadlines. Coding is a craft I picked up out of curiosity, kept out of passion, and still genuinely enjoy every time I sit down to build something.

My main weapon is Flutter, but I refuse to be a one-stack dev. I'm constantly exploring, mastering, and adopting new skills — whether that's jumping deeper into Linux internals, wiring up a backend API, or experimenting with a tool I only discovered yesterday.

My mission is simple: build free, open-source alternatives to existing software that actually respect people. Better UX, real privacy, no tracking, no paywalls.

Outside of code, I'm at the gym, deep in a music rabbit hole, or watching something on Netflix until my brain resets. Probably introverted. Definitely bad at small talk. Great at building things quietly and shipping them anyway.

The future? No roadmap. There are things I want to build that I haven't even imagined yet — and that's the part that actually keeps it exciting. 2026 Furkan has zero plans and zero apologies for it. 🦋
